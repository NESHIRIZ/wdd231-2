Membership levels and benefits

NP Membership (np)

No fee. For nonprofit organizations — access to community programs and networking.

Bronze Membership (bronze)

Entry-level membership with access to events and member directory listing.

Silver Membership (silver)

Includes advertising opportunities, training discounts, and priority event registration.

Gold Membership (gold)

Full benefits: spotlight advertising, premium event invites, and sponsor opportunities.

NP Membership — Benefits

  • Zero membership fee.
  • Access to community programs and volunteer collaborations.
  • Listing in the member directory.

Bronze Membership — Benefits

  • Member directory listing with basic profile.
  • Access to member-only events.
  • Discounted ticket prices for workshops.

Silver Membership — Benefits

  • Priority placement in the member directory.
  • Featured content opportunities (spotlight).
  • Discounts for training and promotional events.

Gold Membership — Benefits

  • Premium spotlight on home page and newsletters.
  • Complimentary sponsor invitations to key events.
  • Dedicated account liaison and marketing support.
